Nearby Places

Sanjay Gandhi National Park

This is a beautiful national park located just 5 km from Borivali Executive Hotel. It is known for its scenic beauty, rich flora and fauna, and various recreational activities such as trekking, boating, and picnicking.

Gorai Beach

This is a popular beach located around 8 km from Borivali Executive Hotel. It is known for its serene atmosphere, clean water, and various water sports activities such as jet skiing and parasailing.

EsselWorld

This is an amusement park located around 9 km from Borivali Executive Hotel. It offers various rides, attractions, and entertainment shows suitable for all age groups.

Kanheri Caves

This is an ancient Buddhist rock-cut cave complex located around 10 km from Borivali Executive Hotel. It is known for its beautiful carvings, sculptures, and paintings that depict the life of Buddha and the Buddhist teachings.
